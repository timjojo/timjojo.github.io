Double-Clocked Mode
When you use the double-clocked mode, only channel 1 is available for sampling. Other channels are not available. In this case, the channel 1 uses full of on-board buffer memory. Actual sampling rate is twice of the rate specified. The number of pre-trigger samples and post-trigger samples can be set up to twice of them at the normal condition. The number of samples, the number of pre-trigger samples and post-trigger samples are must be even. The sampling counter event must be specified by a multiple of 2048, 4096, 8192, 16384, or 32768.
