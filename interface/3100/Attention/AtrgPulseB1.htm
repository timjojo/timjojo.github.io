Analog Trigger Pulse 
When analog triggers satisfy their conditions, pulses are output from the ATRG OUT pin. The pulse doesn't depend on start-trigger and stop-trigger.
When a start-trigger and stop-trigger are specified and satisfies the stop-trigger condition before satisfying start-trigger condition, pulse is output. However, there is no difference in sampling.
