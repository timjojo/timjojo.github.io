Configuring Current Input Ranges
The voltage input range of 0 V to +5 V can be configured as the current input range of 0 mA to +20 mA depending on the settings. The voltage range of +1 V to +5 V can also be configured as the current input range of +4 mA to +20 mA.

-Software Configurations
Specify AD_1_5V for channel 1 and channel 2, and AD_4_20mA for channel 3 and channel 4 according to the ulRange member in the ADSMPLCHREQ structure.  

-Hardware Configuration 
  PCI-3126: Set JP1 and JP2 to A, JP3 and JP4 to B. Set 1and 2 of DSW1 to A, 3 and 4 of DSW1 to B. 
 CTP/CPZ-3126: Set DSW1 and DSW2 to A, DSW3 and DSW4 to B, 1and 2 of DSW9 to OFF, 3 and 4 of DSW9 to ON.
