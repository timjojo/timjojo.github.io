13.5 List of DLL Functions

No. Function Description Applicable Note

1

AdOpen

 Opens an analog-to-digital input board/card and enables to access to the board/card.

-

2

AdClose

Closes the analog-to-digital input board/card and releases the resources. Any subsequent accesses to the board/card are forbidden.

-

3

AdGetDeviceInfo

Retrieves the specifications of the board/card.

 -

4

AdSetBoardConfig

Configures event handing of the board/card.

 -

5

AdGetBoardConfig

Retrieves an event source called on the board/card.

 -

6

AdSetSamplingConfig

 Configures sampling conditions of the board/card. -

7

AdGetSamplingConfig

 Retrieves sampling conditions on the board/card. -

8

 AdBmSetSamplingConfig Configures sampling conditions. 1

9

 AdBmGetSamplingConfig Retrieves sampling conditions. 1

 AdLvSetSamplingConfig Configures sampling conditions of the board/card. 2

 AdLvGetSamplingConfig Retrieves sampling conditions of the board/card. 2

12

 AdAllocateSamplingBuffer Allocates a sampling buffer.   -

13

 AdLvCalibration Runs automatic calibration of the board/card.  2

14

AdGetSamplingData

 Retrieves sampling data from the board/card. -

15

 AdBmGetSamplingData Retrieves sampling data. 3

 AdLvGetSamplingData Retrieves sampling data from the board/card. 2

AdClearSamplingData

 Clears the sampling data in the sampling buffer. -

19

AdReadSamplingBuffer

 Reads sampling data contained in the sampling buffer. 5

AdStartSampling

 AdLvStartSampling Starts continuous sampling on the board/card. 2

AdStartFileSampling

 Starts continuous sampling and saves the data into a data file simultaneously. 5

23

 AdBmStartFileSampling Starts continuous sampling and saves the data into a data file simultaneously. 3

24

 AdTriggerSampling Samples a single channel on the board/card at each trigger asserted on the EXTRG IN pin. (only for the boards/cards whose analog-to-digital input channels are multiplexed into an AD converter and whose data transfer mode is the programmed I/O mode) 6

25

 AdMemTriggerSampling Acquires the specified number of samples at each external trigger assertion. 6

26

 AdSyncSampling Enables you to achieve a synchronous sampling on boards/cards connected in parallel. -

27

AdStopSampling

 Stops continuous sampling performed as an overlapped operation. -

28

 AdLvStopSampling Stops continuous sampling performed as an overlapped operation. 2

29

AdGetStatus

 Retrieves the sampling status on the board/card.  -

30

 AdLvGetStatus Retrieves the sampling status on the board/card. 2

31

AdInputAD

32

AdInputDI

 Reads data from the digital input pins on the board/card. -

33

AdOutputDO

 Writes data to the  digital output pins on the board/card. -

 AdMeasureTemperature Reads temperature of the I/O connector on the board/card. 2

AdAdjustVR

AdDataConv

AdReadFile
